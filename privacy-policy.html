Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PetCare Journal ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the application. By downloading or using PetCare Journal, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. This App is operated by an independent individual developer, not a company or corporation.

2. No Data Collection — Our Core Principle

PetCare Journal is designed with a strict no-data-collection architecture. We do not collect, receive, store, process, transmit, sell, share, or have access to any personal information or data that you enter into the App. All information you input — including pet names, health records, weight logs, event reminders, veterinarian contacts, photos, and documents — is stored exclusively in the local storage of your personal device (iPhone/iPad). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party controlled by us.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect any of the following: — Your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier — Pet health data, medical records, or veterinary information — Device location or GPS data — Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or IDFA — Usage statistics, analytics, or behavioral data — Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to our servers — Photos or files you store in the App — Any content you type or input into the App We have no backend server that receives user data. There is no database on our end that stores anything about you or your pets.

4. Local Data Storage

All data you enter in PetCare Journal is stored locally on your device using standard iOS local storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. You retain full ownership and control of this data at all times. If you enable iCloud Backup in your iOS Settings, your device data — including App data — may be included in your iCloud backup. This backup is managed entirely by Apple Inc. and is subject to Apple's own Privacy Policy (apple.com/privacy). We have no access to your iCloud backups.

5. Camera and Photo Library Access

If you choose to add photos to the App, you will be prompted to grant access to your device camera or photo library. This permission is used solely to let you select or capture a photo that is then stored locally on your device within the App. We do not upload, transmit, or access your photos in any way. You may revoke this permission at any time in your iOS Settings.

6. Push Notifications

PetCare Journal uses Apple's local notification system to send reminders about upcoming pet events (such as vet appointments or vaccinations). These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No notification content is transmitted to us or any third party. You may disable notifications at any time in your iOS Settings.

7. Biometric Authentication

If you enable Face ID or Touch ID to protect the App, biometric data is processed entirely by iOS and is never accessible to PetCare Journal or transmitted to any server. We only receive a boolean result (authenticated / not authenticated) from the iOS system.

8. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

PetCare Journal offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store payment system. We do not receive, process, or store any payment card information, billing details, or financial data. Your subscription status is verified locally on your device through Apple's StoreKit framework. For questions about billing, contact Apple Support directly.

9. Third-Party Services

PetCare Journal does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data-collection SDKs. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Crashlytics, or any similar service that would transmit your data to third parties.

10. Children's Privacy

PetCare Journal is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal information whatsoever, there is no risk of unauthorized collection of children's data. The App is rated 4+ on the App Store and is suitable for general audiences.

11. Your Rights and Data Control

Because all data is stored exclusively on your device, you have complete and sole control over it. Because we do not collect personal data, many rights typically granted under data protection laws (such as data access or deletion requests) are technically unnecessary, as we have no ability to access or process your data. — Access: View all your data directly within the App at any time — Export: Use "Export My Data" in Settings to download a full JSON backup to your device — Delete: Use "Delete All My Data" in Settings to permanently and irreversibly remove all App data from your device — Uninstall: Deleting the App from your iPhone permanently removes all locally stored data We cannot access, retrieve, restore, or delete your data on your behalf because we never have access to it.

12. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend enabling a device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID to protect your iPhone. The App also offers an optional in-app biometric lock for additional protection.

13. Apple Inc. Disclaimer

Apple Inc. is not responsible for the data handling practices of this App. PetCare Journal is an independent application developed by an individual developer. Apple's role is limited to distributing the App through the App Store. Any privacy-related questions should be directed to the Developer, not to Apple.

14. Jurisdiction and Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Poland and applicable EU data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable. Given that PetCare Journal collects no personal data whatsoever, the practical obligations under GDPR are minimal. However, we are committed to full transparency and compliance with applicable law. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. However, since we do not process any personal data, there is no data processing activity to complain about.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. The updated policy will be available within the App. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
